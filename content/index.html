A Neural Algorithm of Artistic Style. [Leon A. Gatys, Alexander S. Ecker, and Matthias Bethge]

I am a second year PhD student at UT Dallas and was a research assistant in Dr. Gans' SeRViCE lab from Fall 2014 through Fall 2015. We collaborated with Drs. Xuejun Gu and Steve Jiang of UT Southwestern's Radiation Oncology lab working on proof-of-concept prototype of a soft-robot actuation mechanism for the head and neck regions during maskless and frameless cancer radiotherapy.

I was a serial TA for Dr. Gans' Introduction to Robotics course from Fall 2014 through Fall 2015. In the spring of 2015, I was the TA for the Masters Level Linear Systems Course.

My research interests lie broadly at the intersection of control systems, and vision/machine learning. I am away from UT Dallas this Spring and Summer.

My mostly academic blog has up-to-date posts on what I think about.
